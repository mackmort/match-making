# Intake Questions

These questions are sent to prospective clients before their intro call.

1. Tell me a bit about yourself. Who you are, what you do, and what matters to you in your daily life.

2. What does your life feel like right now? What feels full, what you'd like to cultivate, and what's been on your mind lately.

3. What does a really good, nourishing day look like for you?

4. How would you describe the way you relate to people more generally? And what do you feel you contribute to the people close to you?

5. What has your dating life looked like recently, and what brought you here?

6. What kind of partnership are you looking for? How do you want to feel in it?

7. What tends to feel more challenging for you in intimate dynamics?

8. What qualities draw you to someone? And what have you learned doesn't work for you?

9. When connection feels good to you, what does it actually feel like?

10. Anything else you'd want me to understand about you as I get to know you?
